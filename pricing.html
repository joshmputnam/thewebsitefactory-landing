FAQs

Find answers to frequently asked questions about pricing, payment options, and our refund policy.

Our landing pages start at $300. The final cost may vary depending on the specific requirements and additional features you choose. Contact us for a personalized quote.

What are the payment options?

We accept major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. We also offer the option to pay via PayPal for added convenience.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we have a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our services, let us know within 30 days of your purchase, and we'll refund your payment.

Can I get a discount?

We occasionally run promotional offers and discounts. Follow us on social media and subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on any ongoing promotions.

How long does it take?

The turnaround time for a landing page depends on the complexity and scope of the project. We strive to deliver high-quality results within a reasonable timeframe.
