Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about The Website Factory's services and landing pages.

Contact
How much does it cost?

Our Starter Plan landing pages start at $300. We use the template of your choosing from any of our starter templates and customize the images, colors, text, and fonts. We can, of course, do much more customization in an upgraded plan, even to the point of building you a completely custom website. If you need more than just a landing page, be sure to ask us about it here!

What are the features?

Our landing pages are designed to be visually appealing, mobile-friendly, and optimized for conversions. On the Starter Plan they include features such as contact forms and basic SEO. On upgraded plans we can add analytics, A/B testing, and even marketing.

Do I need a website?

Yes, having a website is essential for any business in today's digital age. It helps establish your online presence, build credibility, and attract potential customers.

How long does it take?

The time it takes to build a landing page depends on the complexity of the project. We will provide you with an estimated timeline during the initial consultation. For a basic one-page landing page we can often get it finished up within 1 week. It just depends on the situation.

Can I make changes?

Yes, we offer revision rounds to ensure that the final landing page meets your requirements. Additional charges may apply for extensive changes beyond the scope of the initial agreement. Once the page is locked, we can still make changes, but it will incur an additional fee.
